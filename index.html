How much does a Golden Retriever puppy cost?

The average Golden Retriever puppy price of £300 on Pets4Homes reflects a market with significant variation. Prices range from £200 to £3,000, influenced by factors like breeder reputation, puppy lineage, location, and current market demand. Potential owners should focus on breeder quality and health guarantees rather than solely on price.

Comparison by Seller Type

In the UK, Golden Retriever puppy prices differ significantly between KC (Kennel Club) registered and non-registered sellers. KC-registered breeders typically charge higher prices due to strict breeding standards, comprehensive health testing, and documented pedigrees. Non-registered sellers often offer lower-priced puppies, but may lack the same health guarantees and breed verification. The price gap reflects differences in breeding quality, traceability, and adherence to professional breeding standards.
